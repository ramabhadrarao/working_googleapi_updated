Getting Started

Account Creation

To use Route Analytics, you'll need to create an account. Click the "Register" button in the navigation bar and fill out the registration form.

Creating Your First Route

After logging in, click on "New Route" in the navigation bar. You can enter addresses or coordinates to define your route. Select your vehicle type and click "Analyze Route" to get started.

Navigating the Dashboard

The route dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your route analysis. Use the tabs to switch between different analysis categories:

  • Risk Analysis
  • Regulatory Compliance
  • Emergency Preparedness
  • Environmental Analysis
  • Amenities & POIs

Route Analysis

Address Input

You can enter starting and destination addresses in the text fields. The autocomplete feature will help you find specific locations.

Coordinate Input

For precise location input, switch to coordinate mode and enter latitude and longitude values for your starting point and destination.

Vehicle Type Selection

Choose your vehicle type from the dropdown menu. This selection affects various aspects of the analysis, including:

  • Adjusted travel time calculations
  • Vehicle-specific compliance requirements
  • Risk assessment considerations
  • Environmental impact calculations
Alternative Routes

The system automatically analyzes multiple route options. You can compare these alternatives by clicking on the "Alternative Routes" button in the dashboard.

Risk Assessment

Risk Factors

The system evaluates several risk factors along your route:

  • Sharp turns and blind spots
  • Elevation changes and steep gradients
  • Weather conditions
  • Road quality
  • Terrain type (urban, rural, etc.)
Risk Visualization

Risk levels are color-coded on the route map:

  • Red - High risk segments
  • Orange - Medium risk segments
  • Green - Low risk segments
